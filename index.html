The ceremony will begin at 5:30 PM at West University Baptist Church. Cocktail hour and reception to follow at La Colombe D’or. Attire is black tie optional.

Hotel rooms are blocked off under Nathan-Clifton wedding at Hotel ICON. Hotel ICON is adjacent to Market Square in downtown Houston and close to many popular bars and restaurants. Book soon to ensure availability!

Shuttles will be leaving from Hotel ICON at 4:45 and 5:00 to the church. There will be shuttle services from the church to the reception and back to Hotel ICON until midnight. There will be free valet service at the reception venue at the Yoakum Boulevard entrance.
