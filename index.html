FAQ

How do I get there?

Fly into HOU with Southwest, which is closer to town. Or fly into IAH if coming from far, far away or for more flexibility and options.

Where should I stay?

The closest and nicest hotel to the reception is Hotel Zaza where we have a block of rooms, just give them our name!

Another alternative is Hotel ICON downtown, which is a bit cheaper and right across from all the best new bars and restaurants in Houston. Again, tell them we sent you.

Where are you registered?

We would love something that reminds us of you. If you need suggestions, we are registered online at STORE or at Macy's if brick and mortar shopping is more your style.

What's Houston like?

If you can't tell, we love Houston and can't wait to have you as our guest in the city we call home!

We work in the world's largest medical center and love to explore the countless restaurants, bars, music, and cultural events around town. Check out the map above for some recommendations while you're here.
